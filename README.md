# Stock Management and Billing System for "Super Sales" Grocery

[cite_start]A comprehensive digital solution designed to transition grocery stores from manual, handwritten stock management to an automated, integrated platform[cite: 3693, 3720]. [cite_start]This system streamlines inventory control and billing processes to enhance operational efficiency and accuracy[cite: 3740, 3811].

## 🚀 Key Features
* [cite_start]**Inventory Management:** Real-time tracking of stock levels, product categorization, and automated alerts for low stock[cite: 3742, 3779].
* [cite_start]**Efficient Billing:** User-friendly interface for cashiers featuring barcode scanning and multiple payment options[cite: 3722, 3803].
* [cite_start]**Data-Driven Insights:** Comprehensive reporting tools to analyze sales trends, popular products, and stock turnover rates[cite: 3723, 3805].
* [cite_start]**Automated Replenishment:** System-generated reorder requests based on predefined stock thresholds[cite: 3727, 3782].
* [cite_start]**Secure User Management:** Role-based access control for managers and cashiers with secure login authentication[cite: 3785, 3794].

## 📂 Project Structure
[cite_start]The system is built using the Waterfall methodology [cite: 3753] and includes the following key modules:
* [cite_start]**Login Module:** Secure gateway for authorized personnel[cite: 3850].
* [cite_start]**Customer Details:** Management of customer records and history[cite: 3855].
* [cite_start]**Product Details:** Database management for inventory items, manufacture, and expiry dates[cite: 3920].
* [cite_start]**Payment & Sales Details:** Transaction handling and sales record maintenance[cite: 3878, 3950].
* [cite_start]**Supplier Details:** Tracking of supplier information and stock replenishment history[cite: 3973].
